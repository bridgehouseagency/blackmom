About Us

Community Care, Advocacy & Learning

Black Moms NB is a community-rooted organization supporting Black and African-descended children, youth, families, and communities through connection, advocacy, learning, and collective care.

We create spaces for people to share experiences, access support, strengthen community connections, and navigate systemic barriers with guidance and care.

How We Show Up for Community

Four interconnected areas of work — each shaped by lived experience and community need.

💬

Community Conversations & Learning Spaces

Spaces shaped by lived experiences where individuals, families, and community members can gather to share, reflect, learn, and build connection.

🧭

Support & Systems Navigation

Walking alongside individuals, families, and community with advocacy, connection, and support so no family has to navigate systems alone.

📚

Resources & Advocacy Tools

Practical guidance, resources, and accessible tools grounded in lived experiences and community knowledge to empower families.

🏫

Training & Collaboration

Working with schools, workplaces, and organizations to strengthen how they respond and support Black and African-descended families and youth.
