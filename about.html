Community-Rooted.

Rooted in community and advocacy, we support Black and African-descended families in New Brunswick.

Who We Are

Black Moms NB

Black Moms NB is a community-rooted organization supporting Black and African-descended children, youth, families, and communities through connection, advocacy, learning, and collective care.

We create spaces for people to share experiences, access support, strengthen community connections, and navigate systemic barriers with guidance and care.

Our work centers the wellbeing of Black and African-descended children, youth, and families while advocating for fair, responsive, and accountable systems.

Our Work

What We Do

Our work responds to what Black and African-descended families and communities are navigating in real time.

💬

Community Conversations & Learning Spaces

Spaces shaped by lived experiences where individuals, families, and community members can gather to share, reflect, learn, and build connection.

🧭

Support & Systems Navigation

Walking alongside individuals, families, and community with advocacy, connection, and support so no family has to navigate systems alone.

📋

Resources & Advocacy Tools

Practical guidance, resources, and accessible tools grounded in lived experiences and community knowledge.

🏫

Training & Collaboration

Community-rooted learning and collaboration that encourages reflection, accountability, meaningful engagement, and stronger community response.

Working Together

Collaborative by Nature

We collaborate with people and organizations who are committed to meaningful engagement and shared responsibility.

This includes a willingness to listen, reflect, and take action in ways that contribute to more inclusive, responsive, and accountable environments that support Black and African-descended children, families, and communities.
